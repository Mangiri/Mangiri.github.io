Save Our Kids — Who are We?

Save Our Kids is a Non-Profit Organization and for 15 years, we have been giving children in Africa and around the world a healthy start in life, the opportunity to learn and protection from harm. When crisis strikes, we are always among the first to respond and the last to leave. We do whatever it takes to save children, transforming their lives and the future we share.

What we do? — Donate and Save Our Kids

Education

Through our health and nutrition programs, education and emergency relief, Save our Kids is transforming the lives of Africa's children. We helped more than 24 million African children in 2017.

Health

We target the major causes of maternal, newborn and child deaths and provide the best health and nutrition programs to save children's lives and ensure they grow up healthy.Every child deserves a healthy start in life. Thanks to people like you.
